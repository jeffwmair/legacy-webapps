The Costs

To understand the cost, look at the number under "Used". (ignore "overage" and "remaining" numbers) and refer to the list below.

  • $10: between 0 and 150 MB
  • $25: between 150 MB and 1,000 MB (1GB)
  • $35: between 1,000 MB (1GB) and 5,000 MB (5GB)
  • and for anything over 5,000 MB, each additional 1,000 MB costs another $10
